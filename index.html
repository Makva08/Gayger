Sensor monitoring

Executive Summary

  • Radiation has been around us throughout our evolution. Most of it formed naturally from minerals, which are presented in ground, soil, water, and even our bodies. Thus, our bodies are designed to deal with the low levels we're exposed to every day. But too much radiation can damage tissues by changing cell structure and damaging DNA. This can cause serious health problems, including cancer. Our team decided to dedicate our time and funding to researching sophisticated and relatively inexpensive solutions to monitor the radiation level and efficiently analyze the data received.

Our Goals

To design a hardware to monitor radiation level.

To create a software which will analyze the information coming from our device.

Generate appropriate response if hazard is detected.

How it works

  • Your product enables user to measure and monitor radiation level. It comes in several designs, such as: handheld, autonomous solar powered etc. Those options have a variety of features, such as: gps, sim card communication, wifi, display. Features can be added on clients request.

    Information received from the device, is sent to mqtt broker and then to database, to first receive and then display the data. Custom application is responsible to get data from mqtt broker and then store it in database. Django is used to host website and display telemetry data on map. Grafana server provides more detailed measurements from each individual device.
